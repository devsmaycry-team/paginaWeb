Butcher Shop Management System

Commercial system designed for comprehensive butcher shop administration. Allows managing products, sales, customers, credit accounts, orders and profit reports from a single platform.

Netbeans Java Java FX MySql

General Description

Management system oriented towards butcher shop businesses, designed to cover all daily tasks of the business: product loading, stock control, ticket sales and customer administration.

The system allows handling both unit products and meat and poultry by weight, recording sales in real time, calculating daily profits and keeping track of customers with current accounts (credit).

⚙️ System Modules

1

Product Management

Merchandise loading and administration

Complete product catalog administration:

  • Product creation, editing and deletion
  • Products by unit and by weight
  • Price and stock control
2

Point of Sale

Ticket sales and automatic registration

Sales system designed for customer service:

  • Quick sale via ticket
  • Automatic total calculation
  • Registration of each transaction
  • Support for different payment methods
3

Customers, Credit and Orders

Commercial relationship management

Customer management tools:

  • Customer registration
  • Current account management (orders)
  • Advance payment history
