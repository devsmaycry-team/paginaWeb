Sugist – Comprehensive System for Institutions

Academic platform designed for educational institutions. Allows managing programs, technical courses, students, teachers, attendance, grades and academic situations in a single centralized system.

Symfony PHP MySQL Javascript

General Description

Sugist is an institutional management module that centralizes all academic and administrative information. It is oriented towards schools, institutes and educational centers that need to organize their daily work efficiently.

The system allows managing programs, technical courses, study plans, students, teachers and their academic activities. It includes attendance records, grades, midterms, finals and tracking of academic situations.
