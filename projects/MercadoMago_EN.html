MercadoMago – Online Shopping Platform

E-commerce website that allows users to buy and sell products, manage listings, make payments and coordinate shipping in a secure and centralized way.

SpringBoot Angular MySQL TypeScript

General Description

MercadoMago is a marketplace-type platform where users can register, list products, make purchases and manage shipping.

It includes modules for user administration, products, questions and answers, shopping cart, transaction history and seller panel.

⚙️ System Modules

1

User Management

Registration, profiles and roles

Complete account administration:

  • Registration and login
  • Buyer profiles
  • Saved shipping addresses
2

Product Management

Listings and catalog

Listing administration:

  • Product creation, editing and deletion
  • Categories and subcategories
  • Image galleries
  • Stock and product variations
3

Shopping and Shipping

Complete purchase process

Integrated shopping flow:

  • Shopping cart
  • Payment gateway
  • Shipping method selection
  • Order tracking
